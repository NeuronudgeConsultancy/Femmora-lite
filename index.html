The RAS combines hormonal patterns, ovarian reserve markers (when available), lifestyle factors, and medical history to estimate your biological fertility age. The FEMMORA chip is a small, sterile device inserted just under the skin (similar to a contraceptive implant). It continuously monitors key hormone levels and transmits data to your smartphone.
